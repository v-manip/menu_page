V-MANIP

The V-MANIP project provides the organizational frame for:

  • Consulting of the meteorological and hydrographical science communities regarding new and desired ways to visualise and manipulate multidimensional satellite, thematic and auxiliary data, including the output of models; collecting related expectations for the upcoming missions in the Sentinel era;
  • Translation of science community requirements into software system concepts (engineering requirements, architecture design, and validation plan). Inclusion of concepts and specifications from the future operational ngEO system including interface specifications of the visualization pipeline;
  • Determining a project policy regarding access security to services and data;
  • Building the system which can fulfil the functional, interface, performance, and security requirements by re-use, new implementation, integration and testing of software components;
  • Collecting and preparing demonstration datasets (including simulated datasets for future EO missions) with representative heterogeneity and volume for functional and performance testing;
  • Commissioning the system in two-steps:
    1. Prototype System via Factory Acceptance
    2. Demonstrator System which is accessible here
  • Showcasing the final Demonstrator System to the science communities in order to collect validation feedback and recommendations for future service enhancements;
  • Making the newly developed software available as Free and Open Source Software.
